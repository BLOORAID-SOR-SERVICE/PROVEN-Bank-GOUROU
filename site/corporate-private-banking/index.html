A member of the PROVEN Group of companies, PROVEN Bank is a financial services company incorporated in the Cayman Islands and is licensed under the Bank and Trust Companies Act as a Category A Bank. This license allows the bank to conduct banking operations both in the domestic and international markets. With two branches in the Grand Cayman, it provides services to residents and non-residents; offering financial solutions such as – Deposit accounts (savings, chequing, and CDs); Loans (consumer/ personal, mortgage/real estate); credit cards; foreign exchange services; internet and telephone banking.
